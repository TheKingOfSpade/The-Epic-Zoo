About our Armadillos

Habitat

Armadillos live in temperate and warm habitats, including rain forests, grasslands, and semi-deserts. Because of their low metabolic rate and lack of fat stores, cold is their enemy, and spates of intemperate weather can wipe out whole populations.

Arms The Armadillo

Arms is a very bold little fellow. He would roll into the unknown with ease. You can see him do rolling races with his friends.

Zenn the Armadillo

Zenn was just born a year ago. She is the calmest animal in the zoo. You can find her laying around the leaves and watching the other animals do their thing in the zoo.
